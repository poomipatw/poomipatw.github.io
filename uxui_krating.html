Introduction

This project is a development of my earlier "Funder(Fd.r)" work, a case study on a mutual fund investment application. I made the decision to enhance my portfolio case study after working for a well known financial securities company in Thailand so that it would better reflect my current viewpoint and what the project should be for the present and future markets.


Below are the sample of my previous version and research:

Pain Point

In thailand, we are limited toward a single application for internal stock investment which is called "Streaming”. The application is old school and only meant for hardcore trader. The application's design has a high learning curve which pushed the new investors away from starting investing.

Quick Goal

I decided to develop a single stop investment tool for majority of the existing assets with user friendly ux/ui.

Home

As Ux designer, we have to make hypothesis on what user really wants but it is impossible to meet all the user criteria. Thus I decided to implement the widget design for the user. The default home screen fabricated what were hypothesized first but user can personalize their own later on to meet their best usage.

Example down below:

Portfolio

Portfolio is the central screen to view all of your assets. When user enter this screen, they should be able to track down on what they are holding and proving the entrance to all of the assets. It was very challenging here because of the amount of assets type and countries we have here in the application.

Invest

Instead of slapping on the whole stock listing, in each investing asset, we introduced high performance asset, tools, and more for user to choose from.

After working in the industry, I have found out that majority of the user already know what they want to buy. What we can do here is to introduce new asset or new perspective of investing for the user and most important, enough information for the new investor.

Asset Landing

Asset landing includes the essential info for the user. Investing is not like betting on a horse race. We have to ensure that there will be enough information for user to make decision on buying or sell each asset.

The form of information can be the range from very simple summary up until the seconds updating numbers.

Buy Sell

The design for buying and selling requirement was set to be “simple and easy”. Everything will be calculated here, everything will be transparent.

Pop up were used to make sure user can switch back to see the numbers quickly incase they they have to do quick dicision making during trades. The UI is optimized for both new investor and experienced one.
