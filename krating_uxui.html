Introduction

This project is a development of my earlier "Funder(Fd.r)" work, a case study on a mutual fund investment application. I made the decision to enhance my portfolio case study after working for a well known financial securities company in Thailand so that it would better reflect my current viewpoint and what the project should be for the present and future markets.


Below are the sample of my previous version and research:

Pain Point

In thailand, we are limited toward a single application for internal stock investment which is called "Streaming”. The application is old school and only meant for hardcore trader. The application's design has a high learning curve which pushed the new investors away from starting investing.

Quick Goal

I decided to develop a single stop investment tool for majority of the existing assets with user friendly ux/ui.

Home

As Ux designer, we have to make hypothesis on what user really wants but it is impossible to meet all the user criteria. Thus I decided to implement the widget design for the user. The default home screen fabricated what were hypothesized first but user can personalize their own later on to meet their best usage.
